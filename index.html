Meet Paul

An Arkansas native with a passion for helping people move better and live better.

Growing up in El Dorado, Arkansas, Paul discovered his love for physical fitness and helping others through sports. After experiencing firsthand the positive impact of physical therapy during his high school years, he knew he'd found his calling.

Paul pursued his passion at Harding University, where he earned both his Bachelor's degree in Exercise Science (2014) and Doctorate in Physical Therapy (2017). During his time in Searcy, he fell in love with the community and developed his patient-centered approach to care.

What sets Paul apart is his commitment to continuous learning and specialized care. He's pursued advanced certifications in dry needling, LSVT-BIG therapy, manual therapy, and orthopedic specialization - all to provide the most effective, evidence-based treatments for his patients.

When he's not helping patients regain their mobility, Paul enjoys staying active with his family. He and his wife Kacie (married in 2021) are raising two energetic boys and love exploring Northwest Arkansas. You might spot Paul on the pickleball court, biking trails, or basketball court when he's not in the clinic.

"My greatest joy is seeing my patients regain their independence and return to the activities they love. There's nothing more rewarding than helping someone move without pain."
